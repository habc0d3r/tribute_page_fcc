Sadhguru

A Yogi, Mystic & Visionary...

Jagadish (Jaggi) Vasudev famously know as Sadhguru is an Indian Yogi, founder of Isha Foundation .

About Sadhguru

  • Sadhguru was born on 3 September 1957 in Mysore, Karnataka, India.
  • At the age of 13, he started learning Yoga from Malladihalli Raghavendra.
  • He studied at Demonstration School, Mysore and Mahajana Pre-University College.
  • He graduated from the University of Mysore with a bachelor's degree in English.
  • Sadhguru did several successful businesses includig a poultry farm and a construction business.
  • Sadhguru met Vijaya Kumari in 1984 in Mysuru and got married.
  • In 1992, he established the Isha Foundation, an organisation headquartered near the city of Coimbatore.
  • Sadhguru received the Padma Vibhushan, the second-highest civilian award from the Government of India, in 2017 in recognition of his contribution to the field of spirituality.
  • He has written many books includig NY Times best-selling book Inner Enginnering, Death.

Sadhguru's Work for the World & Achievements
